### Org Policies

These are admin-controlled policy flags (enforced server-side where applicable).

* Force attachment encryption validation (reject malformed attachments payload)
* Disable passphrase mode (flag for future)
* Require re-auth for decrypt (recommended)
* Enforce key rotation reminder (days)\
  0 = off. When due, server writes a risk audit entry.
