# privacy

QuantumMail is a privacy-focused browser extension designed to perform client-side encryption and decryption.

<details>

<summary>Data Collection</summary>

QuantumMail does not collect, store, or transmit personal data, email content, or user identifiers.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Data Usage</summary>

All processing occurs locally within the user's browser and is initiated explicitly by the user.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Data Sharing</summary>

QuantumMail does not share data with third parties.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Data Retention</summary>

No user data is retained. Any locally stored configuration data can be cleared by removing the extension.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Contact</summary>

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the project maintainer.

</details>
